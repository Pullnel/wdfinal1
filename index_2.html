ROOM 1

“Societies in the renaissance period”

“Societies in the renaissance period” is built on famous works about the social situation around the world at the most prosperous period of nations. These works best reflect the anger and resentment of the society at that time. The prosperity of the country always comes with the hidden corners of society and the works in this room have faithfully reproduced the sad faces of people in that society.

ROOM 2

“Wars in the age of innovation”

“Wars in the age of innovation” inspired SAM to build this room. After the prosperous chains of empires are always accompanied by wars for territory, chaos in society, competition for titles or greed from people. The works in this room are inspired by that. Through the perspective of an artist, the works are the realization of a declining era, the battles that keep happening have been recorded and clearly depicted through the works here. Each work in this room is associated with the famous battles of that period.

ROOM 3

“The beauty of humanity by the standards”

“The beauty of humanity by the standards” was an indispensable topic in society at that time, that's why this room was created and also the last room in our museum. The beauty of man at that time was the most essential reason leading to the competition between man and man. A girl or a boy or anyone with a standard beauty is the subject of society. Those beauties are the foundation for social norms about people. To this day those standards have always made man the measure of everything on this earth. But we cannot deny that these beauties make us more valuable. The works in this room simply reproduce those existing values as faithfully as possible to remind us of the beauties of that period.
